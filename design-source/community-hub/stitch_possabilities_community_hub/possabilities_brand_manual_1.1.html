06.


Rules of brand use.


We have established the PossAbilities brand guidelines to ensure a consistent brand identity throughout all materials.

These guidelines specify the appropriate fonts, characters, and other supporting elements that should be utilised.

Kindly make sure to follow these guidelines to maintain the uniformity of the PossAbilities brand.

  • Please make sure to utilise only the Avenir font, known as PossAbilities, when preparing documents and other publications. This font is readily available on all Venom Hosted desktops and can be accessed through various 365 programs such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and Powerpoint.

  • The official strap line of PossAbilities is ‘Live The Life You Choose’. Despite seeing publications from other members with different tag lines, it is important to note that only the PossAbilities tag, ‘Live The Life You Choose’, should be utilised.

  • The PossAbilities logo must remain unchanged and not be modified in any manner. The brand toolkit includes various logos that have been created in different styles to suit diverse documents.

  • The Brand Toolkit contains all the necessary resources to effectively manage the brand guidelines and operate on a daily basis. Whether you require a document, poster, or a character performing a specific task, or need assistance with adhering to the brand guidelines, you can simply send your request via email to Digital@PossAbilities.org.uk or scan the QR code below.
